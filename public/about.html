Why Choose Galle

A City Unlike
Any Other

Galle is a jewel of the Indian Ocean — a living, breathing UNESCO World Heritage city where 16th-century Dutch colonial architecture meets tropical island life. It's a city of layered histories, vibrant culture, and extraordinary natural beauty.

A Haven for Long-Term Visitors

The city has grown into one of Asia's most beloved destinations for expats, digital nomads, and long-stay travellers. With a thriving community of international residents, excellent English proficiency, world-class cafés, yoga studios, and a relaxed pace of life — Galle offers an unmatched quality of life at a fraction of European costs.

From the ramparts of Galle Fort you can watch the Indian Ocean at sunset. Within minutes you can reach pristine beaches like Unawatuna, Jungle Beach, and Mirissa. Within an hour lies the incredible cultural triangle of the south.

What to Discover

Life in Galle

🏰

Galle Fort

A UNESCO World Heritage Site — wander cobblestone streets lined with boutique hotels, galleries, cafés, and colonial-era churches.

🌊

Beaches

Unawatuna, Jungle Beach, Hikkaduwa and Mirissa are all within easy reach — crystal waters, golden sands, world-class surfing.

🍽️

Cuisine & Cafés

A thriving food scene ranging from authentic Sri Lankan rice & curry to world-class restaurants, specialty coffee, and healthy cafés.

🧘

Wellness & Yoga

Galle is a wellness hub with yoga retreats, Ayurvedic spas, meditation centres, and a healthy, active community of practitioners.

💻

Remote-Work Ready

Reliable internet, co-working spaces, international SIM cards, and a large community of remote workers and entrepreneurs.

🌿

Nature & Wildlife

Sinharaja Rainforest, whale watching in Mirissa, sea turtle nesting sites, and incredible birdwatching all within easy reach.

🏥

Healthcare

Good quality hospitals, pharmacies, and medical facilities in Galle. Colombo's world-class hospitals are just 2 hours away.

🛒

Easy Living

Supermarkets, local markets, laundry services, banks, ATMs, and everything needed for comfortable day-to-day living.

Visa Information

Visiting Sri Lanka
Long-Term

Sri Lanka offers tourist visas allowing stays of up to 30 days (extendable to 90 days in country). Many long-stay guests renew their visas with regular border runs to India or the Maldives. We are happy to guide you on the most up-to-date visa procedures for your nationality.

📌 Pro tip: Apply online at the Sri Lanka ETA website before arrival for the easiest entry. Contact us and we'll share tips from our experienced long-stay guests.
