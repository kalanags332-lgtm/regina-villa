About the Villa

Your Private Sanctuary
in Paradise

Nestled in a lush tropical garden in the historic city of Galle, Regina Villa is a beautifully appointed private home offering an authentic Sri Lankan living experience for discerning long-stay guests.

With traditional architecture, polished teak furnishings, and a verdant garden alive with birdsong, the villa offers a rare opportunity to slow down and truly immerse yourself in island life.
